Chicken Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Bread
  • Mozerella cheese
  • Thyme
  • Italian herbs
  • Panini maker

Steps

  1. Prepare bread.
  2. Place cheese on the bottom slice of the bread.
  3. Sprinkle tyme and italian herbs on to of the cheese.
  4. Place the chicken on top of the cheese.
  5. Place slice of bread on top of chicken.
  6. Cook then eat.
